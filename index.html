About

Hi there! Thanks for visiting my portfolio. I’m Mark and I’m a full-stack developer based in Melbourne, Australia. I'm currently at the end of my studies at Coder Academy and am excited to begin my professional web development career.

I'm passionate about technology. I believe in facilitating positive change in the world and I am excited by the possibilities of how technology can be used to strengthen community and build a better future.

Coming from a background in engineering I love problem solving and understanding the intricate nature of how things work. My development skills are spread across both frontend and backend development, i enjoy understanding all aspects of developing a full application to production.

My favorite projects are those that require me to push beyond my boundaries and to acquire new skills and knowledge in order to succeed.
